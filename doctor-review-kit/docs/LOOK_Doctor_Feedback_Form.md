# LOOK Doctor Initial Assessment Form

Use these questions in Google Forms, Typeform, or email.

## Section 1: Reviewer Profile
- Name
- Specialty / role
- Hospital / practice
- Do you currently manage kidney transplant patients? (Yes / No)
- How often do you review patient-generated logs before or during appointments? (Never / Sometimes / Often)

## Section 2: Overall Reaction
1. In one sentence, what do you think LOOK is trying to do?
2. Does that problem feel real and worth solving? (Yes / Partly / No)
3. How clinically useful does this prototype feel overall? (1-5)
4. How safe does the current framing feel? (1-5)

## Section 3: Feature Usefulness
Rate each as Useful / Maybe / Not useful:
- Morning check-in
- Medication confirmation and streaks
- Daily symptom / emotional log
- Saved questions for doctor visits
- Uploaded report extraction and summaries
- Trend graphs across multiple days
- Doctor-facing summary export

## Section 4: Clinical Safety
1. What part of the current product feels most risky or easiest to misinterpret?
2. Are any charts, labels, or summaries too strong for a non-clinical app?
3. What wording would you change to make the app safer?
4. What should trigger stronger escalation or clearer warnings?

## Section 5: Workflow Fit
1. Would this help before a clinic appointment? Why or why not?
2. Would this help during a review with a patient? Why or why not?
3. What information is missing that would make it more useful for you?
4. What information is unnecessary and should be removed?

## Section 6: Recommendation
- Would you review this again after one more iteration? (Yes / No)
- If yes, what are the top 3 changes you want next?
- If no, what is the main blocker?

## Final Free Text
- Anything else we should know before taking the next iteration forward?
