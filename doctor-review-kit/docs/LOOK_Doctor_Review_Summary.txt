LOOK Doctor Review Summary
Prepared on 22 March 2026 for initial clinician feedback

LOOK is an iPhone-first transplant support app intended to help patients and caregivers organize medication routines, symptom logs, questions for appointments, uploaded reports, and trend summaries.

Safety position
- LOOK provides education, workflow support, and visit preparation only.
- It is not a diagnostic tool or medical service.
- Any concerning symptom or abnormal trend should still prompt direct contact with the transplant team.

What this build can do
- Morning check-in with green / amber / red states
- Medication confirmation and streak tracking
- Daily log for symptoms, emotional state, and doctor notes
- Question capture for future appointments
- Upload blood reports and prescriptions
- Trend graphs for medications, check-ins, trial scores, labs, and question volume
- Patient-facing and doctor-facing insight summaries
- PDF export of a doctor-oriented summary

What we want feedback on
- Is the symptom and adherence data likely to be useful before or during appointments?
- Which trend views are clinically useful, and which feel distracting or low-signal?
- What would make the doctor summary safer, clearer, or more actionable?
- What should not be shown, inferred, or emphasized in a transplant support app?

Current screens included in review
- Home dashboard with morning workflow and medication adherence
- Insights tab with trend charts, notable-day annotations, and doctor summary
- Ask tab for saving structured questions
- Trials tab for daily symptom and medication logs
- Document upload flow for reports and prescriptions

Suggested review process
1. Watch the short walkthrough video.
2. Review the screenshots for the core flow.
3. Open the feedback form and note what feels clinically useful, risky, or missing.

LOOK is currently a prototype for workflow evaluation. Final privacy, analytics, backend document processing, and clinician-facing safeguards are still in progress.
